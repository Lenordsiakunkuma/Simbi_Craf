About Simbi Craft

Established in 2014, Simbi Craft is a privately-owned business serving the aquaculture, fishery, and tourism industries in Zambia and neighboring countries.

From our full-service workshop in Siavonga, we design, build, repair, and modify a wide range of boats and aquaculture equipment.

Our team combines traditional craftsmanship with modern engineering to deliver solutions that withstand the challenging conditions of African waterways.
